# Product

## Register

product

## Users

MiniShuya is for a desktop user who wants a small, gentle companion while working on Windows. The pet should stay present without covering important content, interrupting focus, or feeling like a web widget floating on top of the desktop.

## Product Purpose

MiniShuya is a Windows desktop pet that provides lightweight companionship today and will later support large-model chat, knowledge-base Q&A, and behavior changes based on the user's work state. Success means the pet feels alive, compact, calm, and useful enough to keep on screen for long sessions.

## Brand Personality

Small, gentle, soft.

The default personality is obedient, warm, and companion-like rather than loud or mischievous. Future personalization can allow different character moods from the right-click menu, but the baseline should remain quiet and pleasant.

## Anti-references

MiniShuya should not look like a large rough doll, a big-headed crude placeholder, a large desktop window, an exaggerated bouncing toy, a cheap pink menu, a generic web card component, or an obviously AI-generated interface.

Menus should not cover the character artwork. Transparent parts of the window that are not occupied by the character or menu should feel intangible and should not interfere with interaction with lower windows.

## Design Principles

1. Stay small and out of the way.
2. Express state through subtle character motion, not noisy UI.
3. Keep system controls compact and familiar.
4. Let the character carry the personality; menus should support it quietly.
5. Design for future intelligence without exposing that complexity early.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` and provide calmer motion for users who are sensitive to animation. Keep menu text readable, maintain visible focus states, and avoid relying on color alone to communicate important state.
